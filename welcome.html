AI Bookmark Sorter Installed!

Pin the extension to your toolbar for quick access.

  1. Click the puzzle piece icon in the Chrome toolbar (top-right)
  2. Find AI Bookmark Sorter in the list
  3. Click the pin icon next to it
  4. The extension icon will now always be visible in your toolbar

First-time setup (if not already done)

  1. Open chrome://flags/#optimization-guide-on-device-model → Enabled
  2. Open chrome://flags/#prompt-api-for-gemini-nano → Enabled
  3. Click Relaunch to restart Chrome
  4. Open chrome://components, find Optimization Guide On Device Model, click Check for update
  5. Wait for the model to download (~22 GB). Check status in chrome://on-device-internals

You can close this tab. Click the extension icon to start sorting!
